My Services

Consultation

It all begins with a conversation. First, we run through your ideas, site requirements, and budget. Based on that exchange, I'll propose a contract, ironing out any outstanding details.

Development

I'll build a unique website that will reflect your purpose, values, services, and aesthetic and communicate them effectively to your audience.

Testing

I perform thorough testing of your site or app prior to the final launch on various browsers, operating systems, and devices to be sure that it is responsive and performs well across all platforms.

Launch

Lastly, the final project will be delivered to you. I transfer the site to your possession with the ability to receive ongoing technical support.
