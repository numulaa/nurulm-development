About me

I am a Software Engineer based in Indonesia. I am proficient in both Frontend development and Backend development. I have extensive knowledge in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.Js, MongoDB, and React. As a software engineer, I craft robust, scalable software with an entrepreneurial mindset and relentless hustle. Driven by a passion for innovation, I focus on improving the customer experience, shaping the digital landscape one line of code at a time.

From a high school blogger to a software engineer.

Hey there, I'm Nurul. Let me share my journey with you

My journey with technology began when I was a child, and I was first introduced to computers at the age of five. From that moment, I was hooked. The fascination grew as I discovered that I could create things on the computer, which led me to explore programming, design, and eventually, web development.

Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in web development, focusing on both frontend and backend technologies. I’ve worked extensively with JavaScript, TypeScript, React, and Node.js, and have developed a passion for animation and immersive experiences using GSAP and Three.js. Along the way, I’ve learned the value of continuous learning and growth, whether through formal education or hands-on project experience.

I’ve had the privilege of working on a variety of exciting projects, from creating apps that automate video promotion for businesses to building a platform that connects people with local tasks to earn money. I’ve also contributed to a nursing home project that aimed to keep families connected with their loved ones. My work at LaunchGood, where I serve as a Senior/Lead Software Engineer, is another significant milestone in my career. In addition to my professional work, I’ve taught hundreds of kids and teenagers programming and math, which has been incredibly rewarding. Sharing my knowledge and helping others grow has become a key part of my journey.

Currently, I’m focusing on building a new online business centered around web development services, leveraging my expertise to help clients create high-quality websites. I’m also expanding my knowledge by delving deeper into the technical aspects of a Senior/Lead Software Engineer role, with a goal to master these skills and continue progressing in my career.

I’m driven by the excitement of solving complex problems and creating innovative solutions. I find joy in sharing knowledge with others, whether it’s through teaching or collaborating with a team. I believe in the power of an abundance mindset and in being a lifelong learner—there’s always something new to discover, and I’m passionate about exploring those opportunities.

Looking ahead, I want to continue growing both professionally and personally. I aim to refine my skills, scale my online business, and become an influential voice in the tech community. My ultimate goal is to continue creating high-value services, make a positive impact, and empower others to reach their full potential.
