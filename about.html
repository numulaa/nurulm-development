About me

I am a Software Engineer based in Indonesia. I am proficient in both Frontend development and Backend development. I have extensive knowledge in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.Js, MongoDB, and React. As a software engineer, I craft robust, scalable software with an entrepreneurial mindset and relentless hustle. From a high school blogger to a software engineer.

Hey there, I'm Nurul. Let me share my journey with you

My love for technology began when I was just three years old. I’d watch my dad work on a computer, mesmerized by how clicks and keystrokes could make things happen. That fascination grew, and eventually, I realized I could make things happen too—starting with my own website to share my poetry.

The turning point came in 8th grade when I built my first blog. I wanted to share my poetry, but instead of waiting for someone to give me a platform, I created my own. That’s when I discovered coding as a powerful tool that blended creativity and problem-solving. In 2018, I dove back into coding while balancing school, and I’ve been hooked ever since.

One of my proudest projects is the Shoplist app I built for my family. It solved a recurring problem: my mom forgetting what we asked her to buy. From day one, it worked like magic, eliminating misunderstandings. That’s the impact I aim for—solving real-world problems with simple, meaningful tech.

Another defining moment was when I led a project to fund scholarships for farmers’ children. We gathered spare coins and managed to fund scholarships for three students. It taught me how small contributions can create big change.

I also contributed to a nursing home project where we built apps for the elderly, their families, and administrators. It reminded me that while aesthetics are important, true success comes from understanding and addressing real user needs.

What sets me apart? I combine creativity and storytelling with logic and problem-solving. Whether it’s designing user-friendly interfaces, seeing things from someone else’s perspective, or finding creative solutions, I’m always up for the challenge. Fun fact: I’ve competed in poetry slams, and I’m skilled in archery and storytelling. You never know when unexpected skills will spark the next big idea.

If there’s one thing I want you to remember, it’s this: I’m eager to learn, driven to create impact, and committed to delivering unexpected value. My goal is for my work to make life easier, more joyful, and meaningful for others.

I’m Nurul Mukhlisa, and I’d love to bring my creativity, problem-solving, and passion for technology to your team. Let’s create something unforgettable together.
