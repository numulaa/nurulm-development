From a high school blogger to a software engineer.

Hey there, I'm Nurul. Let me share my journey with you

Back in high school, I was exploring different interests, just like any other teenager. But what set me apart was my passion for blogging. Instead of the usual teenage gossip, I found myself pouring my thoughts and poetry onto the digital page. It was my little corner of the internet where I could express myself freely.

In 2020, a group of friends and I decided to take on a business plan competition. Our idea? A marketing agency. And guess what? I was the one who took charge of building and managing our website. Armed with determination and a willingness to learn, I dove into the world of web development, teaching myself the ins and outs of WordPress along the way.

Managing the agency's website was both challenging and rewarding. It gave me firsthand experience in the world of technology and sparked my curiosity about what else I could create.

Then came 2021, a year that changed everything. That's when I discovered coding, and let me tell you, it was love at first sight. From the moment I wrote my first line of code, I was hooked. It felt like unlocking a whole new world of possibilities, and I couldn't get enough.

It wasn't all smooth sailing, though. It was challenging, frustrating even, but I kept at it. I took online courses, joined coding communities, and slowly but surely, things started to click.

Now, as I stand here, reflecting on my journey, I see how far I've come. From a high school blogger to the one who built and managed a marketing agency's website to becoming a software engineer, each step has shaped me in profound ways. And while the road ahead may be uncertain, one thing is clear: I'm ready to tackle whatever challenges come my way, armed with determination and a genuine love for all things tech.
